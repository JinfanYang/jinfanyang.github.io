Jinfan Yang PhD Student University of British Columbia

Hi there, I am Jinfan Yang, a PhD student at the University of British Columbia, supervised by Prof. Alla Sheffer. Before I was a CS graudate student of NYU. My research interests span across topics in Digital Geometry Processing. Recently, I have been particularly interested in Computational Manufacturing, Image Processing, Decomposition, Nueral Geometry Representation, and Perceptual.
